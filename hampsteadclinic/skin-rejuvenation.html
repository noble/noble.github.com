Skin Rejuvenation - peels

A chemical peel is a procdedure that is used to improve the texture of the facial skin. Peels are essentially used to remove wrinkling and can help with dry pigmented skin as well. It is carried out by the application of a chemical to the skin that causes the old skin to gradually slough off. The new skin is thus smoother and less wrinkled. There are many different types of chemical peels on the market and you will be individually assessed to decide the appropriate one for you. Often you may need multiple treatments to obtain the desired result.
