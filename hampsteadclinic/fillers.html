Fillers & Lip Augmentation

Whether you want to banish those give away lines, create more sensuous looking lips or simply lose that tired look, the answer lies in Restylane®, the UK's leading, instant aesthetic treatment. Treatment effects can been seen immediately and last around a year.

Restylane combats the signs of ageing

Youthful skin is naturally rich in a substance called hyaluric acid (HA) which binds water to keep skin looking healthy, hydrated and firm.

But as we age, our HA becomes less efficient, leading to loss of support in the skin, loss of firmness and the appearance of lines.

Restylane is an injectable form of pure HA that closely resembles the body's own natural HA.

When Injected into the skin, Restylane counters the visible effects of ageing.

Restylane revitalises looks - in less than an hour

Restylane gives your looks an immediate boost, smoother, healthier, more youthful-looking skin.

Restylane can also restore high cheek bones or correct a weak, receded chin.

Restylane has the added advatages of stimulating collagen production and imporving skin elasticity.

Restylane treatment usually takes no more than 30 minutes. You can see results instantly and it's long lasting too: from 6 to 9 months in cheek/chin and around a year in facial lines.
